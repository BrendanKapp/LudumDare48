Click Tools > RockStudio > RockStudio, the RockStudio window will open up.

Press 'Generate' at the bottom of the window and a gizmo will appear in the scene view. 
Move it to the location where you want to spawn a rock and press down the left button of your mouse. T
his will create a rock object in the scene.


The full documentation can be found here.

https://alexanderameye.github.io/rockstudio/
